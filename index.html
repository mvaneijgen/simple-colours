There are to many colours, so I dumbed down the 16 million HEX colours.

I've only listed the colours that can be created with three charecters, to make them easy to remember

Making decisions on colours had always been difficult. Even more if there are 16 milion of them. This is why I dumbed them down to only 4000 colours. Normaly HEX take up six character from 0-9 and or A-F. Here I made the list of every HEX colour that you could make with only three characters. The colours are now much easier to overlook and easier to remember.
